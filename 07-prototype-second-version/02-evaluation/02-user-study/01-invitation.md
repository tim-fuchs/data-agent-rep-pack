## English Version

Hi [name],

Thank you very much for wanting to participate in the evaluation study of my AI agent!

**Procedure:**

- I have prepared an AI agent that is connected to Jupyter Lab on the Maxwell cluster as well as to multiple knowledge sources (EuXFEL GitLab, GitHub, a RAG database, and the internet).
- You will explore the different ways of interacting with the agent, learn about its features, and test its limits.
- The study will take place on-site at EuXFEL on my computer. You do not need to bring your own device.

**Preparation (optional):**

- You may want to think in advance about a Jupyter-related use case you would like to try with the AI agent.
- If you would like, you can also bring your own Jupyter Notebook to test the AI agent with.
- If you know someone who should also participate in the study, please let me know (also in advance).

**Data processing:**

- During the session, I will take handwritten notes. At the end of the session, we will review them together.
- The AI agent is connected to an externally hosted Large Language Model (LLM). The data sent to the LLM will not be used to train the model. If necessary for data privacy reasons, it is possible to switch to an LLM running locally on my laptop. However, performance would be significantly reduced.

**Scheduling:**

- The session will last approximately 45 to 60 minutes.
- Do you have any preferred times on [Day], [Date]?
- I am available [TimeRange].

Kind regards,
Tim
